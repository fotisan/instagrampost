SEO CLARITY
Lesson 01

Myth: “You need a blog to rank on Google.”

For service businesses, Google just wants to know what you do, for whom, and where.

Lesson 02

Search Intent is everything.

People search “Therapy Austin”, “DUI Lawyer”, “Personal Trainer”. Not “blog posts”.

Lesson 03

Start with 3 core Service Pages.

One page = one problem solved. Keep it simple. That’s a “clean signal” for Google.

Lesson 04

6–8 FAQs = Invisible SEO strategy.

Speak naturally. Answer specific questions to build trust and capture long-tail search.

Lesson 05

Place keywords in “Quiet” spots.

Page Titles, sub-headers, Service lists, and FAQs. Don't force them into your poetic headlines.

Lesson 06

Trust is a ranking factor (indirectly).

When users stay, read, and click, Google sees that “this site is helpful”.

Lesson 07

Make the CTA feel effortless.

“Check availability”, “Book a call”. Avoid generic “Contact” buttons—they feel like a chore.
